Frequently Asked Questions

Explore our FAQs to find answers to common questions, so you have all the information you need to make informed decisions about your educational journey.

What are the requirements to enroll in your courses?

To enroll in the "Introduction to Data Analytics Seminar," you must have a high school diploma, GED, or equivalent certification.

To enroll in all other courses, you need to meet the following requirements set by the Texas Workforce Commission (TWC):

  • Must be at least 18 years of age.
  • Must hold a valid Social Security Number (SSN).
  • Must have a high school diploma, GED, or equivalent certification.
  • Knowledge of Data Analytics fundamentals — a minimum of 40 hours of formal instruction, 3–6 months of employment in a data analytics role, or completion of the "Introduction to Data Analytics Seminar." Completion of the seminar is required if you are new to data analytics.
How much does the Introduction to Data Analytics Seminar cost?

The cost of the "Introduction to Data Analytics Seminar" is $1,999. By paying the entire fee upfront and early, you may be eligible for an early-bird discount of $199. We also provide flexible payment and financing alternatives, as well as scholarship opportunities to help reduce any financial barriers.

Are there any software and hardware requirements?

To create an effective online learning environment, you need access to a computer with a minimum of 8GB RAM — either a Windows system or a Mac running a Windows operating system. A functional camera and microphone are also necessary. Active participation is crucial: you'll need to remain visible to the class via camera and contribute to discussions throughout the program.

Can I enroll if I am an international student?

Certainly! International students are welcome, provided they meet certain requirements.

Introduction to Data Analytics Seminar: Successful completion of high school (public, private, or homeschool) or a recognized high school equivalency credential, as defined by Texas Education Code §61.003.

Job Readiness Program / Interview Prep Bootcamp:

  • Must be at least 18 years old.
  • Must possess a valid Social Security Number (SSN).
  • A high school diploma, GED, or equivalent certification from your country is required.
Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?

Absolutely! We have a range of scholarships and financing alternatives. Whether you're eligible for military scholarships, prefer loan options, or are interested in an ISA (Income Share Agreement), we provide flexible choices. For details, visit our Payment Options page.

Does Colaberry guarantee job placement?

Introduction to Data Analytics Seminar: The seminar's purpose is to introduce you to the field and provide foundational knowledge and skills for advanced instruction, while also being useful in your current job. It does not result in a license or occupational credential.

Job Readiness Program / Interview Prep Bootcamp: Although Colaberry cannot guarantee job placement, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional training and assisting students throughout their job search. We go beyond the course duration — providing a lifelong partnership in your career development.
